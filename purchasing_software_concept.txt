
PURCHASING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM CONCEPT
Based on PRM-12 Purchasing Procedure (ISO 9001:2015 aligned)

------------------------------------------------------------
1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW
------------------------------------------------------------

The Purchasing Management System digitizes the entire procurement lifecycle of the company.
It ensures that the purchasing process follows the official PRM procedures including:

1. Purchase Requisition Slip (PRS) submission
2. Quotation evaluation
3. Purchase Order (PO) creation and approval
4. Supplier coordination
5. Delivery and receiving of materials
6. Payment processing
7. Supplier accreditation and evaluation

The system also provides transparency, audit tracking, and document management.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. USER ROLES
------------------------------------------------------------

REQUESTER
Examples: Project Management, Warehouse, QA/QC, Logistics, Safety
Permissions:
- Create PRS request
- Track request status
- View quotation evaluation
- Confirm supplier selection

PURCHASING ASSOCIATE
Permissions:
- Receive PRS
- Evaluate request
- Request quotations from suppliers
- Prepare Quotation Evaluation Matrix
- Generate Purchase Orders
- Coordinate deliveries
- Maintain procurement records

WAREHOUSE ASSISTANT
Permissions:
- Receive delivered materials
- Record delivery
- Upload Delivery Receipt (DR)
- Upload Sales Invoice (SI)
- Report defective materials

OFFICE OPERATIONS HEAD (OOH)
Permissions:
- Review quotation matrix
- Approve Purchase Orders
- Approve petty cash requests below 10,000 PHP

VP FOR OPERATIONS / PRESIDENT
Permissions:
- Final approval of Purchase Orders
- Approve petty cash above 10,000 PHP
- Decide supplier conflicts

ACCOUNTING DEPARTMENT
Permissions:
- Process supplier payments
- Verify supplier accreditation documents
- Issue Expanded Withholding Tax (EWT)

LOGISTICS DEPARTMENT
Permissions:
- Manage pick-up schedule
- Track delivery logistics

3. CORE SYSTEM MODULES
------------------------------------------------------------

MODULE 1: PRS MANAGEMENT

Purpose:
Digitize the Purchase Requisition Slip process.

Features:
- Create PRS
- Auto-generate PRS reference number
- Input materials, quantity, required date
- Upload technical specifications
- Submit to purchasing

Validation Rules:
- Missing details cannot be submitted
- Requests after 4:00 PM counted next working day

Database Table: PRS

Fields:
PRS_ID
Reference_Number
Requester_Name
Department
Project_Name
Date_Submitted
Required_Date
Status
Remarks


------------------------------------------------------------

MODULE 2: QUOTATION EVALUATION MATRIX

Purpose:
Compare supplier quotations.

Features:
- Record up to 3 supplier quotations
- Compare price, lead time, discounts
- Show recommended supplier
- Send to requester for confirmation

Database Table: QUOTATIONS

Fields:
Quotation_ID
PRS_ID
Supplier_ID
Price
Lead_Time
Discount
Alternative_Item
Evaluation_Result

------------------------------------------------------------

MODULE 3: PURCHASE ORDER MANAGEMENT

Purpose:
Generate and approve Purchase Orders.

Features:
- Auto generate PO number
- Convert PRS into PO
- Send PO for approval
- Digital approval workflow

Approval Workflow:

Purchasing Associate
↓
Office Operations Head
↓
VP Operations

Database Table: PURCHASE_ORDER

Fields:
PO_ID
PRS_ID
Supplier_ID
PO_Number
Date_Created
Total_Amount
Approval_Status
Delivery_Date
Remarks

------------------------------------------------------------

MODULE 4: DELIVERY AND RECEIVING

Purpose:
Track delivery and warehouse receipt.

Features:
- Record delivery
- Upload DR and SI
- QA/QC inspection
- Accept or reject materials

Database Table: DELIVERY

Fields:
Delivery_ID
PO_ID
Supplier_ID
Delivery_Date
Received_By
Inspection_Status
Remarks

------------------------------------------------------------

MODULE 5: PAYMENT PROCESS

Purpose:
Manage supplier payments.

Payment Types:
- Credit Terms
- Online Transfer
- Cash Payment (Petty Cash)

Rules:
Under 10,000 PHP → OOH Approval
Above 10,000 PHP → VP Approval

Database Table: PAYMENTS

Fields:
Payment_ID
PO_ID
Supplier_ID
Payment_Type
Payment_Status
Amount
Date_Paid

------------------------------------------------------------

MODULE 6: SUPPLIER MANAGEMENT

Purpose:
Manage supplier accreditation.

Features:
- Supplier accreditation form
- Upload documents
- Verify certifications
- Approve supplier

Database Table: SUPPLIERS

Fields:
Supplier_ID
Supplier_Name
Address
Contact_Person
Phone
Email
Accreditation_Status
Payment_Terms

------------------------------------------------------------

MODULE 7: SUPPLIER EVALUATION

Purpose:
Evaluate supplier performance annually.

Evaluation Criteria:
- Delivery time
- Product quality
- Cost
- Customer service
- Compliance

Only suppliers with more than 3 POs annually are evaluated.

Database Table: SUPPLIER_EVALUATION

Fields:
Evaluation_ID
Supplier_ID
Year
Delivery_Score
Quality_Score
Cost_Score
Service_Score
Remarks
